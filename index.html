About

I am an undergraduate at Northeastern University studying Behavioral Neuroscience. I have been part of the Action Lab for over three years, where I study human motor learning.

Goals

In fall 2015, I will begin a MRes in Bioengineering at Imperial College London. I plan to pursue a PhD in bioengineering to continue my research in motor learning. My goal is an academic research career.

Interests

My recent research and courses have led to interests in programming, teaching, computational neuroscience, modeling, and robotics. I also enjoy curling, eating cheese, and writing novels.
