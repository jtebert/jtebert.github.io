About

I recently graduated from Northeastern University with a BS in Behavioral Neuroscience and minor in Computer Science. I have been part of the Action Lab for four years, where I study human motor learning.

Goals

In the fall, I will begin a MRes in Bioengineering at Imperial College London as a Marshall Scholar. I plan to pursue a PhD in bioengineering and continue my research in motor learning in an academic research career.

Interests

I am interested in human motor learning, robotics, and the interface between the two, such as robotic prosthetics, biomimetic robotics, and artificial intelligence.
