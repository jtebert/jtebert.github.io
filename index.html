About

I am an undergraduate at Northeastern University studying Behavioral Neuroscience. I have been part of the Action Lab for three years, where I am involved in research on human motor learning.

Goals

When I complete my bachelor's degree in 2015, I plan to pursue a PhD in neuroscience or bioengineering to continue my research in motor learning. My goal is an academic research career.

Interests

In addition to motor learning, my recent research and courses have led to interests in programming, teaching, computational neuroscience, modeling, and robotics.
