About

I am a PhD candidate in computer science at Harvard University studying bio-inspired collective robotics with Prof. Radhika Nagpal. I also hold an MRes in bioengineering and BS in behavioral neuroscience.

Goals

During my PhD, my goal is to apply concepts from neuroscience and biological learning to improve the collective behavior of robots. After my PhD, I intend to pursue a career in academic research.

Interests

I am interested in biology, robotics, and the interface between the two, such as bio-inspired robotics, cooperative robot control, and artificial intelligence. I also enjoy baking cinnamon rolls.
