About

I am currently pursuing a PhD in computer science at Harvard University studying collective bio-inspired robotics with Prof. Radhika Nagpal. I also hold an MRes in bioengineering and BS in behavioral neuroscience.

Goals

During my PhD, my goal is to apply concepts from neuroscience and biological learning to improve the collective behavior of robots. After my PhD, I intend to pursue a career in academic research.

Interests

I am interested in motor learning, robotics, and the interface between the two, such as biomimetic robotics, cooperative robot control, and artificial intelligence. I also enjoy baking cinnamon rolls.
